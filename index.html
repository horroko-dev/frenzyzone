Why This Duo Works

Unholy Synergy

Physical devastation meets elemental fury. The Frenzy Barbarian carves through single targets and tanks the frontline while the Javazon annihilates packs with lightning and piercing javelins.

Frenzy Barbarian

  • Massive single-target physical DPS with dual-wield attacks
  • Battle Orders provides +life and +mana to both players
  • Natural tank with high life, defense, and life leech
  • Howl scatters trash mobs, letting Zon focus fire
  • Boss killer — Frenzy shreds act bosses and superuniques
+

Java Amazon

  • Lightning Fury melts entire screens of monsters instantly
  • Charged Strike has highest single-target DPS in the game
  • Covers Lightning Immune gaps with physical jab damage
  • Valkyrie and Decoy provide additional tanking/distraction
  • Crowd control queen — clears density the Barb can't reach

Combined Strengths

Battle Orders buffs both characters' survivability. The Barb tanks and kills bosses while the Zon devastates crowds. Together they cover physical and lightning damage types, only struggling against rare Physical + Lightning Immune combos.

Act by Act

Leveling Path

From Blood Moor to the Worldstone Chamber. Follow this timeline to optimize skill allocation and progression for both characters.

Levels 1 – 15

Normal Acts I – II

Frenzy Barbarian

Skills

  • Bash1 pt
  • Double Swing6+ pts
  • Howl1 pt
  • Shout1 pt

Strategy

Pump Strength for gear. Double Swing carries hard — zero mana cost at level 9+. Lead the charge, tank hits. Use Howl to manage packs.

Java Amazon

Skills

  • Jab1 pt
  • Power Strike6+ pts
  • Poison Javelin1 pt
  • Inner Sight1 pt

Strategy

Power Strike is your bread and butter early. Throw normal javelins at range for safety, close in for Power Strike on elites. Save skill points after 12 for Lightning Bolt.

Levels 15 – 24

Normal Acts III – V

Frenzy Barbarian

Skills

  • Frenzy1+ pts (lvl 24)
  • Double SwingMax
  • Battle Orders1 pt (lvl 24)
  • Iron Skin1 pt
  • Increased Speed1 pt

Strategy

Transition to Frenzy at level 24 — everything changes. Battle Orders comes online at the same level, buffing both of you massively. Double Swing until then.

Java Amazon

Skills

  • Lightning Bolt5+ pts
  • Charged Strike1 pt (lvl 18)
  • Power StrikeMax
  • Decoy1 pt

Strategy

Charged Strike at 18 is a massive power spike — bosses evaporate. Lightning Bolt handles ranged clear. Drop Decoy to tank while you throw from behind.

Levels 25 – 40

Nightmare Acts I – III

Frenzy Barbarian

Skills

  • Frenzy10+ pts
  • Battle Orders5+ pts
  • Double SwingMax
  • Taunt1 pt
  • Battle Command1 pt (lvl 30)

Strategy

Frenzy is your main skill now. Keep BO leveled — both of you benefit hugely. Battle Command at 30 gives +1 all skills. Start looking for runeword bases.

Java Amazon

Skills

  • Lightning Fury1+ pts (lvl 30)
  • Charged Strike10+ pts
  • Valkyrie1 pt (lvl 30)
  • Pierce1 pt

Strategy

Lightning Fury at 30 is the biggest power spike in the game. Entire screens die. Focus Charged Strike for bosses, LF for everything else. Valkyrie adds another tank body.

Levels 40 – 60

Nightmare IV – V / Early Hell

Frenzy Barbarian

Skills

  • FrenzyMax
  • Double SwingMax
  • Battle Orders10+ pts
  • Sword/Axe Mastery10+ pts

Strategy

Max Frenzy, then pump synergies and mastery. You're the boss killer — let the Zon clear to the boss room, then you both unload. Keep Crushing Blow gear handy for Act bosses.

Java Amazon

Skills

  • Lightning FuryMax
  • Charged StrikeMax

  • Valkyrie1+ pts

Strategy

Max LF first, then CS. Lightning Strike synergizes both. You're the clear speed — throw LF into packs and watch the chain lightning cascade. Titan's Revenge is your holy grail.

Party Play

Duo Strategies

Coordination tips to maximize your duo's efficiency from Normal through Hell.

I

The Barb Leads, the Zon Clears

Let the Barbarian run ahead to draw aggro and tank. The Javazon follows 1-2 screens behind, throwing Lightning Fury into the packs that swarm the Barb. This prevents the Zon from getting surrounded.

II

Battle Orders is Non-Negotiable

Always rebuff BO before dangerous areas. The Javazon benefits massively — she's naturally squishy, and BO can double her life pool. Barb should cast BC first, then BO for maximum duration.

III

Splitting Immunities

Lightning Immune? The Barb handles it with physical damage. Physical Immune? Extremely rare, but the Zon's lightning cuts through. The only nightmare: dual-immune Physical + Lightning. Skip those.

IV

Boss Kill Protocol

For act bosses: Barb tanks and uses Frenzy with Crushing Blow gear. Zon uses Charged Strike at melee range — it's the highest single-target DPS skill in the game. Bosses melt in seconds.

V

Optimal Farming Zones

Cows: Zon paradise — LF one-shots entire herds. Chaos Sanctuary: Barb tanks Diablo, Zon clears seals. Baal waves: perfect duo content, rotate tanking and clearing by wave type.

VI

Exp Sharing Strategy

Stay within 2 screens of each other for shared XP. In 2-player games, monsters have 50% more HP but you gain full XP. The faster clear speed more than makes up for the HP increase.
